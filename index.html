About Me.

Hello! I’m an Environmental Science graduate (BSc) from Noakhali Science and Technology University (NSTU), Bangladesh, currently working as a Research Assistant in Environmental Quality Assurance Laboratory, NSTU. I’m interested in working with Remote sensing and GIS to solve environmental issues like climate change, ecosystem service loss, degradation of natural resources etc. Till now, I have 8 publications in peer-reviewed journals, where I have worked collaboratively with researches from Harvard University, Curtin University, University of Calgary etc.

Currently, I’m working with GIS modeling and Machine learning. Apart from research, I have volunteered for a number of national and international organizations like British Council Bangladesh, Democracywatch, Quantum Foundation, and Coastal Environmental Network (COEN).

Research Interest.

  • GIS and Remote sensing
  • Geo Data Science
  • Hydrology
  • Ecosystem Service
